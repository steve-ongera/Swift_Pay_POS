Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here are some common questions asked by users of our POS system. If you have any other questions, please feel free to contact us.

  • Q: How do I reset my password?

    A: To reset your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

  • Q: How do I generate a sales report?

    A: To generate a sales report, go to the 'Reports' section from the main dashboard. You can filter the reports by date range, product, and other criteria. After filtering, click 'Generate Report' to download it.

  • Q: How do I add a new product to the inventory?

    A: Navigate to the 'Inventory' section and click 'Add New Product'. Fill in the product details such as name, price, quantity, and product category, then click 'Save' to add the product to your inventory.

  • Q: How can I view my transaction history?

    A: To view your transaction history, go to the 'Transactions' section. You can filter transactions by date or product, and view detailed information for each transaction.

  • Q: How can I contact customer support?

    A: You can contact our customer support via email at support@example.com or by calling 123-456-7890. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you with any issues.

Troubleshooting Tips

If you're facing issues with the system, try the following solutions:

  • System is running slow:

    Check your internet connection. A slow or unstable connection can cause delays in transactions. If the issue persists, try refreshing the page or restarting your browser.

  • Error during product checkout:

    If you encounter an error during checkout, verify that all fields are filled correctly. Ensure that the product details and payment information are accurate. If the error continues, contact support for assistance.

  • POS terminal not responding:

    Make sure the terminal is powered on and properly connected to the network. Check the cables and network settings. If the issue persists, reboot the system or contact support for further assistance.
